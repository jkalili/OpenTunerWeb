FAQ

What permission does OpenTuner need?
Microphone access only.

Why microphone access?
To detect your instrument's pitch in real time for tuning.

Do you store recordings or personal data?
No. Audio and user data are not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Privacy Policy

OpenTuner requests microphone access only to provide tuning functionality.

Microphone audio is processed on your device in real time and is not recorded, stored, or sent to servers.

Data Collection

  • OpenTuner does not collect personal information.
  • OpenTuner does not require account creation.
  • OpenTuner does not sell or share user data.
  • OpenTuner does not store tuning or microphone data.

Third-Party Services

OpenTuner does not include third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

Children's Privacy

OpenTuner does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
