Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

OpenTuner is an iOS app that helps you tune your instrument by listening to the sound through your device’s microphone.

Microphone access

OpenTuner requests access to your microphone only to hear the sound of your instrument and detect its pitch for tuning. Audio from the microphone is processed on your device in real time and is not recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

Data collection

OpenTuner does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No account is required and nothing is transmitted to our servers or third parties.
